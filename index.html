Yang About me {CV}

I am a Ph.D. Candidate in Computer Science at the University of Minnesota (UMN), advised by Prof. Changhyun Choi. I am a member of Choice Robotics Lab. Before graduate school, I received my bachelor's degree from Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST).

My research interests lie in robotics, computer vision, and machine learning. In particular, I am interested in intelligent robots that connect human users and the real-world environment.
